The labour force participation rates is calculated as the labour force divided by the total working-age population. This indicator is broken down by age group and it is measured as a percentage of each age group.
OECD (2016), Labour force participation rate (indicator). doi: 10.1787/8a801325-en (Accessed on 11 February 2016)
